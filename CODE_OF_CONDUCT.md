# Code of conduct

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free project community. Be constructive, assume good
faith, and focus criticism on ideas and code rather than people.

Do not disclose another person's private information or any customer, facility, or operational
data. Maintainers may remove content or participation that threatens safety, privacy, or a
welcoming environment.

Report conduct concerns through a private channel provided by the repository host. Maintainers will
review reports promptly, protect confidentiality where possible, and act proportionately.
